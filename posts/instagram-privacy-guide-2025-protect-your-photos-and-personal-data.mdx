Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)</h3> <p>Two-Factor Authentication adds an extra layer of security, ensuring that even if someone gets hold of your password, they can’t access your account without a second form of identification.</p> <h4>How to Enable 2FA:</h4> <ul> <li> <p><strong>Mobile</strong>:</p> <ol> <li>Open the Instagram app and go to your profile.</li> <li>Tap the three horizontal lines in the top right corner.</li> <li>Select <strong>Settings</strong>, then tap on <strong>Security</strong>.</li> <li>Choose <strong>Two-Factor Authentication</strong> and toggle it on.</li> <li>Follow the prompts to choose your method (SMS code or authentication app).</li> </ol> </li> <li> <p><strong>Desktop</strong>:</p> <ol> <li>Go to Instagram.com and log in.</li> <li>Click on your profile picture, then select <strong>Settings</strong>.</li> <li>Click on <strong>Security</strong> and then <strong>Two-Factor Authentication</strong>.</li> <li>Click <strong>Edit</strong> and follow the steps as prompted.</li> </ol> </li> </ul> <h3>2.
